About the Project


With all of the chaos that is happening on the stock market right due to social media problems, we thought it would be great for traders, investors and companies to be able to see if their stocks are being talked about on social media! We can't underestimate the power of using social media trends to predict stock prices. If more people are talking a certain stock, it may be a good idea to see what they are saying and make an informed decision!
